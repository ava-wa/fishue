Fishue: The Water Conservation App for You

In countries all around the globe, communities combat water scarcity and treasure every drop. By playing our part to conserve water, we can sustain the world's freshwater supply and support their efforts, no matter the distance between us.

A water-efficient person uses 80-100 gallons of water, or less, per day. Are you meeting the standards?

Fishue can help you reach your goals!

Fishue is an app that is available on all electronic devices from phones to tablets. Fishue can help you conserve water with a snap of a finger. Its easy setup will give you accurate statistics on your water consumption from your faucet, showerhead, or appliance in no time, after just one simple test to find the output rate. It’s an intuitive, accessible, and useful tool to manage your impact on the planet.

Key Features:

Every time you shower, use the dishwasher, or wash produce, set a timer and Fishue will calculate your approximate water consumption and report the statistics back to you.

With our intuitive visual interface, we let you know in a glance whether you’re achieving your customized goals for water saving, and how you can decrease your water waste.

You’ll get a score for how eco-friendly you are, which you can compare with your friends and neighbours! And every day that you meet your goals, we’ll keep your streak and incentivize the water-saving process so that you continue to achieve.

Fishue also includes a timer to help you regulate the time you spend on showering, brushing your teeth, or more.
