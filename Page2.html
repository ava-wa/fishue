    Did you Know?
  • You lose an about of 10 gallons of your water footprint to leaks daily. If you can't install more water-efficient fixtures, you can save the planet by repearing leaks.
  • If you use a low-flow showerhead, you can save up to 15 gallons of water every time you take a 10-minute shower.
  • Every minute you shave off using your use of hot water, you can save energy and keep dollars in your pocket.
